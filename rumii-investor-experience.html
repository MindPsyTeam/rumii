The Drift Pattern

Disengagement is gradual,
behavioural, and detectable.

Four behavioural stages, weeks apart. Sleep fragments, social contact thins, routine breaks down — long before a missed appointment or a dropout. The signal is there.

Weeks 1–2
Stable engagement
Regular patterns, consistent activity, predictable contact.
Weeks 3–5
Subtle behavioural shift
Sleep disruption, fewer social contacts, more phone pickups.
Weeks 6–8
Drift accelerates
Isolation, routine breakdown, declining adherence.
Week 8+
Formal disengagement
Dropout, missed appointments, loss to follow-up.

The signal is there. The infrastructure to detect and act on it is not. That is what Rumii builds.
